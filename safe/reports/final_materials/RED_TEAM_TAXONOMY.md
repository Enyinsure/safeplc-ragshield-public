# Red Team Attack Taxonomy

## A. Prompt Injection

- OCR instruction injection
- System prompt exfiltration bait
- Role hijacking

Typical target: make the RAG layer ignore safety rules or reveal hidden instructions.

## B. Evidence Poisoning

- Forged safety rule
- Forged vendor patch
- Forged maintenance instruction
- Forged universal wiring conclusion

Typical target: make a false industrial statement look like manual evidence.

## C. Risk Confusion

- Real alarm evidence plus poisoned sentence
- Real wrong wiring diagram plus malicious interpretation
- Fault diagnosis page disguised as bypass advice

Typical target: exploit the fact that real manuals contain warning and fault language.

## D. Audit Tampering

- Modify query
- Modify answer/action
- Modify evidence page
- Modify evidence hash
- Modify M-EPI decision
- Modify signature

Typical target: alter the accountability trail after a trusted query has been answered.
